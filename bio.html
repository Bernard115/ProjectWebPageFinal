About Me!

My name is Bernard Hasimaj, I am a 3rd year student at Toronto Metropolitan University majouring in business technology management. I have a passion for business, sales, marketing and information technology. I have a drive for learning new things and applying them in a manner that gives me a sense of purpose. With a solid foundation in business analysis tools, and a keen eye for detail, I am confident and eager in gaining a position that is looking for my skills, knowledge and passions.


My Background

I was born on May 21st, 2002 in Vlore, Albania. I came to Canada at the age of 11 and moved to Toronto at 15. Despite the major climate differences, I easily grew acustomed to Canada and am thankful for the opportunities that Canada has given me. From a small age I have noticed Toronto grow more reliant on information technology with smartphones becoming a multipurpose device for day to day living and WiFI networks being available every street you walk through. I would say that this city has not only affected my standards of living, but has also influnced my interests in post-secondary education.
